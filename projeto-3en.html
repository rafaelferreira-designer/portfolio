Temple Clothes

Branding

With the arrival of the Professional Aptitude Exam, we are faced with a final project. This project is the one that gathers the technical skills taught during the three years of course. For the same I chose an area of personal taste, and at the same time an area that I felt comfortable.

Temple Clothes is a brand specialized in urban clothing, always having as objective the satisfaction of its customers. Currently Temple Clothes manufactures from T-Shirts to Hoddies with the so-called "Streetwear" style that covers the elements of sportswear and urban. The brand intends to instill professionalism, dedication and modernization, in search of the latest trends and innovations in products so that customers always get the highest quality possible.

This project was carried out in several areas of work, from the creation of the logo, with the respective identity manual, to the idealization and creation of clothing, a website built in wordpress for exhibition and sale of clothing. A photographic session was also performed with the clothing and for the exhibition of the photographs a Lookbook of the Fall/Winter 19/20 collection was created.

To promote the Temple Clothes project, several social networks were created from Instagram, Facebook and Youtube to cover the entire target audience. Instagram and Facebook were used for the exhibition of clothing, and Youtube was used for the dissemination of the promotional video.

With this project I obtained a rating of 17 values.
